My Journey

I grew up humbly in a rural town with 4x the national average violent crime rate, and a teen pregnancy rate that was 50% greater than the state’s average. I became financially independent at 18, powered through Wall Street roles in investment banking and as a stock picker, did corporate turnarounds, and became the Chief Financial Officer for a $100+ fintech company at the age of 36. But I had to teach myself about personal finances along the way.



At 37, I walked away from it all, at the pinnacle of my career. Why? Because I don’t believe life is about amassing material things while having to answer your bosses’ text at 5am on a Sunday. With my help, you’ll be able to make the big pivot you’ve always wanted through a “F**k You Fund”, and have the empowerment, choice, purpose, and opportunity that can only come with it. In other words, you will be able to follow your dreams and find yourself through financial freedom.
